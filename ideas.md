**Animation**: Subtle scan-line effects on section transitions, product specs counting up on scroll entry, breathing glow on the ClawPool icon.

**Typography System**: Space Grotesk (display, 800 weight) + JetBrains Mono (specs/technical) + Inter (body). Aggressive size contrast between headlines and body.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Particle/star field background in hero section
2. Glowing border accents on feature cards
3. Animated connection lines between feature icons

**Interaction Philosophy**: Every interaction should feel like activating a system. Hover states reveal hidden information. Scroll triggers system boot sequences.

**Animation**: Staggered entrance animations, glowing pulse on the product icon, data stream effects on spec reveals.

**Typography System**: Syne (display, ultra-bold) + Space Mono (specs) + Inter (body). The contrast between futuristic display and readable body creates the right tension.

## Selected Design: Option 3 — Cyberpunk Hardware Showcase (Deep Space Edition)

This approach best matches the ClawPool brand — a hardware product that lives in the dark, glows with intelligence, and feels like it belongs in a serious developer's setup. The deep space aesthetic reinforces the "secure habitat" positioning, while the data-visualization aesthetic makes the technical specs feel exciting rather than dry.
